



*** PrizmatixLED8Set_A.csv ***

A set of 8 LEDs that were selected after brute-force search over all combinations of the LEDs in the PrizmatixLEDFullSet.csv, with the choice guided by maximizing contrast for a set of modulations.

The specific spectra in here are after the LEDs pass through the set of dichroic filters that are illustrated in the schematic PDF and listed on this webpage.
